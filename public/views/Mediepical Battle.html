Mediepical Battle

Mediepical Battle is a game made with the Arduino technology for a better experience in the imersion.
The controllers consist of a rod, a bow and a shield, which one the three players must use for attacking each other or defend, until only one remains in the battlefield. The knight is the only that must defend until time ends, while the others win by eliminating with magic and arrows.
Game developed by Bernardo Alvarez Braga, Lucas Nunes and Matheus Nunes.
Technologies used in this game: Unity, Arduino Uno.
