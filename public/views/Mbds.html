A vibrant game about captivating the whole party with your wild dance moves! Show off your skill and keep the dance vibe alive to achieve the highest score!
This game was developed at Global Game Jam 2018.

Programming by Bernardo Alvarez Braga, Leonardo Tagliaro e João Batista Manique Henz.
3D models by Alexandre S. da Silva Jr.
2D art by Giovanni Ramos Tagliaro.
Sound design by Jéssica Lorencetti.
