Medieval Battle

Medieval Battle is a multiplayer turn-based rpg where the players must defeat hordes of enemies.
It's possible to chose between the classes mage and warrior, which one with unique skills.
Technologies used in this game: HTML, CSS, Javascript, MaterializeCSS, AngularJS, Phaser, Node.js and Socket.io.
Programação por Bernardo Alvarez Braga, Gabriel Fazenda e Matheus Nunes.
