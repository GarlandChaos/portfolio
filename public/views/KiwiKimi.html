In a forest, lives a kiwi that dreams of having wings to fly like other birds. The other animals make fun of him, but a mysterious figure can change everything by giving him a magical amulet that can make his dream a reality. With renewed hope, the kiwi departs in a journey of self-discovery.

Programming, narrative writing, game design and 3D art by Bernardo Alvarez Braga.

2D Art by Bruna da Silva Souza.
